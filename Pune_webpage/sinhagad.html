Sinhagad Fort

About.....

Sinhagad Fort, a historic hill fortress near Pune, Maharashtra, was a strategic site for the Maratha Empire. Perched on a steep cliff, it witnessed numerous battles, including the famous Battle of Sinhagad in 1670. The fort's rugged terrain and fortified walls made it a formidable stronghold. It features significant landmarks like the memorial to Tanaji Malusare and ancient temples. Constructed in 1732, it witnessed significant historical events and served as a political center.
