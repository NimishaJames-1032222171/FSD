Urban Foundry

About.....

Urban Foundry is a trendy restaurant and bar that combines industrial-chic decor with a lively atmosphere. It is a popular spot for both dining and nightlife. The restaurant offers a fusion of Indian and international cuisines, with a menu that includes small plates, mains, and a variety of beverages. Urban Foundry is known for its unique industrial-themed interiors, complete with exposed brick walls, metal fixtures, and eclectic furniture.
