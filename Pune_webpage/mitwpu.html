MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), located in Pune, Maharashtra, is a distinguished institution dedicated to fostering academic excellence and global peace. Established in 1983 under the Maharashtra Academy of Engineering and Educational Research (MAEER), MIT-WPU has evolved into a prominent university offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines such as engineering, management, law, liberal arts, science, and commerce.
