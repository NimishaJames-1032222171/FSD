Shaniwarwada

About.....

Shaniwarwada, an 18th-century fortification in Pune, Maharashtra, was the seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire. Constructed in 1732, it witnessed significant historical events and served as a political center. The fort's architecture showcases Maratha grandeur, with a majestic entrance, the Delhi Darwaza, and intricate design elements. Despite a fire in 1828 that destroyed much of its interior, the site retains historical importance.
